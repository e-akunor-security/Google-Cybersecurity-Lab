==========================================================
             THREAT HUNTING: SIGNS OF INFECTION
==========================================================
Analyst: Emmanuel Akunor
Subject: Indicators of Compromise (IoCs)

1. NETWORK: Unexpected outbound traffic on ports 4444, 8080.
2. PROCESSES: Unknown binaries running from /tmp or /var/tmp.
3. AUTH: Multiple "Failed Password" entries in /var/log/auth.log.
4. FILES: Sudden appearance of .encrypted extensions (Ransomware).
5. SCHEDULING: New, suspicious entries in 'crontab -l'.
