==========================================================
             NETWORK DISCOVERY & TARGETING
==========================================================

1. FINDING YOUR OWN IP:
   Command: hostname -I
   (Used to tell the attacker where to connect).

2. TESTING CONNECTIVITY:
   Command: ping [Target_IP]
   (If this fails, the firewall or network is blocking you).

3. REMOTE CONNECTION:
   Command: nc [Target_IP] [Port]
   (The real-world version of the Netcat attack).

NOTE: Both laptops MUST be on the same Wi-Fi/Router 
for this to work without advanced configuration.
