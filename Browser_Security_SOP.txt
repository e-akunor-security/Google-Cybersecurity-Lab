==========================================================
             BROWSER SECURITY & PRIVACY
==========================================================

1. OFFICIAL SOURCE:
   Only download Chrome/Brave/Edge from the official 
   .deb links. Never from "Free Software" blogs.

2. EXTENSIONS TO USE:
   - uBlock Origin: Blocks malicious ads and trackers.
   - Privacy Badger: Stops sites from spying on you.

3. COOKIE SAFETY:
   Clear your "Session Cookies" after doing 
   cybersecurity lab work to ensure no data leakage.

4. THE "SANDBOX" ADVANTAGE:
   Browsers are safer than apps because they are 
   "sandboxed"—restricted from touching your system.
==========================================================
