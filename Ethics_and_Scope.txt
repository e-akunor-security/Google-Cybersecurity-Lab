==========================================================
             CYBER LAB: RULES OF ENGAGEMENT
==========================================================

1. CONSENT: 
   Never test on a device without the owner's permission.
   (Even if it's your wife, or she might change the Wi-Fi 
   password on you!)

2. NO DESTRUCTION: 
   In a "Closed Port" scan, we observe. We do not 
   brute force or delete files.

3. DATA PRIVACY: 
   If a scan reveals a password or personal file, 
   the analyst must stop and report it.

4. THE "REVERSE" RULE:
   If ports are closed, the only ethical way in is 
   through a "User-Authorized" reverse connection.
==========================================================
