==========================================================
             NMAP STATE INTERPRETATION
==========================================================

1. OPEN:
   The service (like Netcat) is running and waiting for 
   you. The "door" is wide open.

2. CLOSED:
   The firewall is NOT blocking you, but the program 
   isn't running. You're knocking on an empty house.

3. FILTERED:
   A firewall is blocking the scan. Nmap can't even 
   tell if the "door" exists.

4. UNKNOWN:
   Usually means Nmap is confused by the response. 
   Try adding the "-sV" flag for more detail.
==========================================================
